AGE Atlas allows you to explore tissue- and condition-specific correlations between biological age and your gene, gene set, or biological function.

It works by aggregating public gene expression datasets from NCBI GEO and annotating these samples with age, tissue, disease state, etc. This allows you to determine correlations between age and gene expression on a large scale across many experimental conditions.

AGE Atlas is currently in pre-publication, alpha status! If you are reading this, you've (hopefully) been given advance access because you are a collaborator or interested party on the OUHSC campus. Please keep the results confidential (i.e., within OUHSC/OMRF) until we have published. Since it is an active research project, the results are subject to change as we improve our algorithms and incorporate more datasets.

Your feedback is greatly desired! We are committed to improving this tool to make it as widely useful as possible. Please send any comments or suggestions to gilesc@omrf.org. In addition to feature requests or suggestions for interface improvements, we also want to know if you experience problems from particular browsers or mobile devices.
